Sky Cao

 

I am an NSF postdoctoral fellow and C.L.E. Moore Instructor at MIT. Previously, I was member in the School of Mathematics at the Institute for Advanced Study. I received my PhD from Stanford University, advised by Sourav Chatterjee. I am interested in probability, analysis, and stochastic geometric analysis – in particular, Yang–Mills, singular SPDE, random surfaces.

Starting July 1, I will be Assistant Professor of Statistics and Data Science at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania.
